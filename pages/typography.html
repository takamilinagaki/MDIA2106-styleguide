Typography

Meddy uses Tilt Neon for the logo and Quicksand as the typeface throughout the app. Tilt Neon carries 2 valuations and Quicksand carries 5 valuations. These fonts were chosen for its ease of readability.

Main Typography

  • Logo:

    • Tilt Neon 50px Regular

  • Header:

    • Quicksand 25px Semibold

  • Texts:

    • Quicksand 20px Regular

  • Description:

    • Quicksand 15px light
